4 JMB men arrested
Police arrested four suspected members of a faction of banned extremist outfit Jammatul Mujahedeen Bangladesh raiding different areas of Durgapur and Puthia upazilas in Rajshahi early Friday.

The arrested are identified as Aslam, 37, Mohammad Ali, 30, Md Babu Huzur, 28, and Abul Kashem Rokon, 38.

Sumit Chowdhury, additional superintendent of Rajshahi special branch police, told New Age that a team of police conducted an anti-extremist drive in the two upazilas of the districts and arrested them along with some weapons and books that propagate extremism.

Two cases were also filed in these connections with respective police stations, he added.
