80kg venison, two deer heads, skin seized in Bagerhat
Members of Bangladesh Coast Guard seized a trawler with 80 kilograms of venison, two deer heads and a skin from Sunderbans at Katlar Canel of Sarankhola upazila in Bagerhat on Thursday night.

Tipped-off, a team of coast guard conducted a drive in the area and recovered the venison, heads and skin from the trawler around 7:00pm, said staff officer Lieutenant Commander M Fariduzzaman Khan of Mongla west zone (operation) of coast guard.

However, no one was arrested as the poachers managed to flee the scene sensing presence of the coast guard personnel, he said.
