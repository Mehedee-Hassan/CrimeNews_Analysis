b'BNP men may behind abduction of Farhad Mazhar: Hasan Mahmud..BNP men may behind abduction of Farhad Mazhar: Hasan Mahmud.\nAwami League publication and publicity secretary Hasan Mahmud on Thursday said that the activists of Bangladesh Nationalist Party might be behind the abduction of poet, writer and columnist Farhad Mazhar.\n\nWhile speaking at a discussion at National Press Club, Hasan said that Farhad in his statement in the court said that any vested quarter might be behind the abduction to embarrass the government.\n\nFarhad was abducted on Monday early morning when he came out of his Shyamoli residence to buy medicine.\n\nThe law enforcers rescued Farhad on Monday late night from a Dhaka bound bus of the Hainf Paribahan at Noyapara of Abhaynagar in Jessore.\n\nThe BNP leaders were frustrated when the law enforcers rescued Farhad Mazhar safe, Hasan said.\n\nThe BNP leaders wanted to wage movement on the issue of the of the abduction, he added.\n\nHasan, also the spokesman of Awami League, called for investigating the issue properly if the BNP leaders were behind the abduction.\n\nNAP Bhashani organised the discussion.'
