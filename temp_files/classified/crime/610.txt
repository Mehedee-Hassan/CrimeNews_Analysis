b'Three Khagrachari govt pry schools closed fearing landslide..Three Khagrachari govt pry schools closed fearing landslide.\nDighinala upazila administration on Wednesday closed three government primary schools for indefinite period apprehending landslide due to incessant downpour.\n\nThe schools are \xe2\x80\x94 Mayafa Para Government Primary School, Khetrapur Government Primary School and Chomrachhari Primary School.\n\nSheikh Shahidul Islam, the upazila nirbahi officer, visited several schools in the upazila in the afternoon and decided to close three of those to avoid casualties due to its risky condition.\n\nAmong the three schools, two were built on the hill where there are two hundred students, 33 of which are PSC examinees, said upazila administration sources.'
