b'Five suicide bombers attack Lebanese army during raids..Five suicide bombers attack Lebanese army during raids.\nFive suicide bombers attacked Lebanese soldiers as they raided two Syrian refugee camps in the Arsal area at the border with Syria on Friday and a sixth extremist threw a hand grenade at a patrol, the army said. Seven soldiers were wounded.\n\nA Lebanese army statement said that one suicide bomber had detonated his explosive laden belt in front of an army patrol during a manhunt for suspected extremists in a refugee camp in the north-eastern border town.\n\nThe army said that four other suicide bombers blew themselves up without causing any injuries among soldiers, while another extremist threw a grenade at a patrol, injuring four soldiers.\n\nDuring the raids an explosive device blew up while four other explosive devices that were being prepared by the extremists were defused.\n\nThe Lebanese army has in recent months stepped up raids in the makeshift camps built on the edge of the town where tens of thousands of impoverished Syrian refugees who had fled violence in their country in recent years live in squalid conditions.\n\nThe camps around the town have long been a haven for extremists coming from Syria who have clashed with Lebanese forces conducting security raids in search of suspects hiding among the refugees.\n\nThe extremists briefly overran the town in 2014 in a battle that killed dozens and marked one of the more serious spillovers of the Syrian conflict in Lebanon.\n\nA number of attacks in Lebanon in recent years have been linked to the war in Syria, where Lebanese Shi\xe2\x80\x99ite group Hezbollah is fighting in support of president Bashar al-Assad.'
