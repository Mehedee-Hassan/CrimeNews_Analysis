of aubergine increased by Tk 20-40 a kg and the item was retailing at Tk 60-100 a kg depending on its quality.\n\nThe price of green chilli increased by Tk 40 a kg and the item was selling at Tk 80-100 a kg in the city markets on Sunday.\n\nCoriander leaves price increased by Tk 80 a kg and the spice was retailing at Tk 200-220 a kg on the day.\n\nCucumber price increased by Tk 10-20 a kg and the item was retailing at Tk 40-60 a kg in the city markets.\n\nBesides, traders on Sunday were not following the beef price set by the Dhaka South City Corporation and were selling the item at Tk 500-530 a kg in the city.\n\nThe DSCC has recently set the price of beef at Tk 475 a kg for the month of Ramadan, which is Tk 55 higher than the last year\xe2\x80\x99s price.\n\nDespite the unexpected hikes in the commodity prices, the government monitoring was seen absent at the markets in the city on the first day of Ramadan.\n\nCommerce minister Tofail Ahmed on May 23 gave guarantee that the
