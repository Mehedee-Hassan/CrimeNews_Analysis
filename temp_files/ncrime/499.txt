b'CNG stations to remain open 24 hours..CNG stations to remain open 24 hours.\nThe Compressed Natural Gas filling stations will remain open round the clock until July 3 on the occasion of Eid-ul-Fitr for uninterrupted movement of the vehicles, said Titas Gas Transmission and Distribution Company Limited in a press release.\n\nThe decision was taken for the benefit of the common people who have been travelling on the occasion of the Eid.\n\nThe release, however, said that the CNG filling stations under the company would remain closed for 24 hours since 12:00am past the day after Eid for renovation works of Bibiyana gas field.'
